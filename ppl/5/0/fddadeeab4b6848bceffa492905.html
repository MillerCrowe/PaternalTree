Mr. L.C. Draper

Dear Sir:

In 1718 John Hoisington came to that part of Wallingford, now known as Meriden. Whence he came or where he went (the supposition is to Heaven) or how long he remained here, whom he married, or whether he married at all, I am unable to state, as no record remains.

Yours Respectfully,
Chas. H. Davis
