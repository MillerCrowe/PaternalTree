Note: 3 (Bio)

Alberta Pearl Hoisington
Alberta Pearl Hoisington was born on October 10, 1894, in Winterset, Iowa, USA. She was the daughter of Albert Lincoln Hoisington and Edith May Hamilton. Alberta's parents divorced in 1903 when she was about 9 years old. She and her sisters stayed with their father, and they moved to Lennox. Her mother remarried soon after the divorce. Alberta's father remarried in 1908, when she was 14. She and her sisters were then raised by their stepmother.
Alberta reportedly had an unhappy childhood. In 1905, she was listed in an Iowa census as living with her father, her younger sister, and Eva Davis (her eventual stepmother), in Walnut, Iowa. Her father, Al, was said to have had a drinking problem and seems to have disappeared around 1915.
Alberta married Henry E. Miller on October 10, 1911, in Winterset, Iowa. She was 17 years old at the time. Alberta and Henry had several children: Elvira Luella, Lillian Ada, Alvin Harold Porter, Vernon Lynn Keith, Donald Roland Addison, and Robert Dale Vincent Miller.
Alberta Pearl Hoisington Miller died on July 18, 1977, in San Antonio, Texas, USA. She was buried in Sunset Mausoleum Memorial Park in San Antonio, Texas.
