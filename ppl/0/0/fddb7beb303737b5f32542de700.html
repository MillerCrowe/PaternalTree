Note

from: Line- Buus.docx
By Trent Miller

Antje was born on 16 October 1845, also in Ostfreisland, and she died on 11 January 1947.

Hendrik came to America as a young man. The family moved to Holland IA in 1870 then to the Dakota Territory in 1877 (homestead 6 miles SW of Lennox SD). He farmed for 4 years NE of Tea SD. In 1908 Hendrik and Antje moved into Lennox SD. Antje came to America in 1863 when 18. She worked in a Freeport IL hotel until her marriage. Antje died at age 101 years, 2 months and 26 days. At the time of her death she was survived by 6 of her 11 children, 57 grandchildren, 139 great grandchildren and 19 great, great grandchildren.

Children of Hendrik & Antje (Fonken) Buus:
